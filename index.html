Is it an illness?
Schizophrenia
Schizophrenia affects how people think, feel, and act, sometimes making it hard to tell what's real from what's not. Imagine hearing voices or believing things that don't match reality â€“ that's part of it. It can also make thinking and talking confusing. Sometimes, feelings can be muddled too. Schizophrenia typically appears in late adolescence or early adulthood, although it can occur at any age. Treatment for schizophrenia often involves a combination of antipsychotic medications, psychotherapy, and support services. Early intervention and ongoing care are important for managing symptoms and improving quality of life. It's not simple, but with open hearts, we aim to make a difference, one understanding step at a time.
